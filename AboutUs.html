ABOUT US

FitWithDivyansh is focused on providing world-class fitness services to service members and civilians in order to educate and promote a lifestyle dedicated to excellence. All revenue generated through FitWithDivyansh will drive our commitment to service by providing charitable donations to other non-profits and causes, assisting soldiers and families in need, and through our own scholarship fund - the FitWithDivyansh Scholars program.
